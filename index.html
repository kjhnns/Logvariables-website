The Variables Approach

01

Forgiving by Design

Move away from Goals (Pass/Fail) to Variables (Data points). A low number is just data, not a failure. No guilt, no broken chains.

Record what matters to you without friction. Custom data types, flexible tracking. Understand your patterns before setting targets.
